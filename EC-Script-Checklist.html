  • Polite/welcoming greeting
  • Introduce yourself
  • Use correct Brand
  • Offer assistance or identify the interest of the caller.
  • Have the customer confirm their name, address, zip code
  • IQ's
    • Ask all Admin Questions to identify the customers needs
    • Provide the Price
    • If accepted, explain scheduling
  • LTRS
    • Explain what is included.
    • Provide the price
    • If accepted, explain scheduling
  • MQ
    • Ask all necessary questions
    • Explain the quote and acceptance process
    • If accepted, explain scheduling

Objection Handling:

  • Acknowledge the concern (Rapport)
  • Ask probing questions (when necessary)
  • Make an attempt to change the customer's mind, ideally by positioning the value of the service or platform.
  • Added services: Explain the 48-hour scheduling policy and get understanding.
  • New Properties: Remind of the Long Grass Policy
  • Ask the customer if they need anything else done while we're out there, providing at least two examples of other services.
  • If accepted, cover Details and Expectations as necessary.
  • Summarize added services
  • Service Window: (1 day aligned with mowing, except for LT/LTRS)
  • Pricing
  • Next Steps (MQ)
  • Offer additional support; "Is there anything else I can help you with today?"
  • Thank the customer for calling "Brand".
